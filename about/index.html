What is LearnFinance?

LearnFinance is a free educational resource covering all FINRA licensing exams for broker-dealer professionals. Whether you are just starting your career in the securities industry or advancing to a principal-level role, LearnFinance provides the study materials you need to prepare with confidence. The site includes comprehensive study guides, practice quizzes, flashcards, and progress tracking — all completely free.

How to Use This Site

  1. Browse Exams — Explore our catalog of 18 licensing exams organized by entry, representative, and principal levels.
  2. Study Topics — Dive into detailed study materials broken down by exam topic and content area.
  3. Take Practice Quizzes — Test your knowledge with timed practice quizzes that mirror the real exam format.
  4. Use Flashcards — Reinforce key concepts with interactive flashcards for each exam.
  5. Track Progress — Monitor your study sessions, quiz scores, and overall readiness over time.

Exams Covered

LearnFinance covers the following 18 licensing exams:

  • SIE — Securities Industry Essentials
  • Series 3 — National Commodities Futures
  • Series 4 — Registered Options Principal
  • Series 6 — Investment Company / Variable Contracts Representative
  • Series 7 — General Securities Representative
  • Series 9/10 — General Securities Sales Supervisor
  • Series 24 — General Securities Principal
  • Series 27 — Financial and Operations Principal
  • Series 50 — Municipal Advisor Representative
  • Series 52 — Municipal Securities Representative
  • Series 53 — Municipal Securities Principal
  • Series 63 — Uniform Securities Agent State Law
  • Series 65 — Uniform Investment Adviser Law
  • Series 66 — Uniform Combined State Law
  • Series 79 — Investment Banking Representative
  • Series 82 — Private Securities Offerings Representative
  • Series 86/87 — Research Analyst
  • Series 99 — Operations Professional
