Series 52

Municipal Securities Representative

The Series 52 exam qualifies individuals to act as municipal securities representatives. It tests knowledge of municipal bond types, underwriting practices, trading, tax considerations, and Municipal Securities Rulemaking Board (MSRB) regulations. The exam is required for professionals who engage in municipal securities activities with public customers, including sales, trading, and financial advisory services related to municipal issuances. Passing the SIE exam is a prerequisite.
