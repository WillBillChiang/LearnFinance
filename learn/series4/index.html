Series 4

Registered Options Principal

The Series 4 exam qualifies candidates to supervise options trading activities at broker-dealer firms. As a Registered Options Principal (ROP), you are responsible for approving options accounts, supervising trading and sales activities, ensuring compliance with exchange and OCC rules, and overseeing margin and position limit compliance. This principal-level exam requires a representative-level registration as a prerequisite.
