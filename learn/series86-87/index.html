Series 86/87

Research Analyst Qualification Examination

The Series 86/87 examination qualifies candidates to function as research analysts at FINRA member firms. The exam is divided into two parts: Part 1 (Series 86) tests analysis competencies including financial statement analysis, valuation methodologies, and quantitative concepts, while Part 2 (Series 87) covers research report writing, regulatory requirements, and communication with the public. Research analysts play a critical role in the capital markets by producing independent, objective research that helps investors make informed decisions. This course covers all testable topics across both parts, from SEC filings and ratio analysis to DCF valuation, comparable analysis, and FINRA Rule 2241 compliance.
